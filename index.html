About Me

Hi, I’m Austen! I’m a web developer from Toronto. My creative process is guided by four principles: Listening before speaking. Whiteboard before keyboard. Consistency before speed. Clarity before cleverness.

As a full-time Web Development Mentor at Juno College, I've received praise from students for the empathy with which I guide them to solutions and a nuanced understanding of foundational concepts. I am known for helping people feel cared-for by sticking with every individual until their overwhelming obstacles are tamed and they feel confident enough to carry on.

Among my technical skills are version control and collaboration with Git, pixel perfect styling with SASS and CSS frameworks, dynamic content with JavaScript, jQuery and React.js, NoSQL with Firebase, responsive web design, and web accessibility. I also build REST APIs with Python, Flask-RESTful and SQL.

If you have any questions, send them to hi@austenvalentine.com.
